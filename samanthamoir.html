We all love a good market! Come along on Saturday 13 October from 3pm - 6pm, free entry. A community market for all, on the Toowoomba Christian College sports oval, with plenty of hard-standing parking available. With over 25 market stalls, food vendors, a coffee van plus children's activities and FREE face painting for the children, why wouldn't you!
