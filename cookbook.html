About

The Best Night In Cookbook

The Best Night In Cookbook is an exploration of our beautiful city, through Toowoomba’s abundance of eateries/eating establishments. Inspired by local passionate head chefs across the Darling Downs, we are excited to bring you the first edition of The Best Night In Cookbook! Full of mouth-watering recipes from some of Toowoomba's most popular cafés & restaurants.

    The Best Night In Cookbook gives you the joy of preparing a meal for friends and family, so rather than dining out, you can enjoy casual dinners and intimate suppers at home for the best night in!
    The Best Night In Cookbook is now available for pre-order until 31 August 2018. Priced at just $20 during pre-order, including a free bonus recipe. Postage & packing extra if required. RRP after pre-order $30.
    The cookbook will be available to purchase from 9 September 2018 from a number of retailers in Toowoomba, as well as right here. The book will be priced at $30.
    All profits raised from the sale of the cookbook are being donated to the Girls' Night In campaign for Cancer Council Queensland. They provide funding, research and support services for women's cancers throughout South West Queensland.

    Blackbird and Finch are our partners for The Best Night In Cookbook. They have been kind enough to financially contribute to the creation of this book, as well as share their insight into the beautiful city we call home.
