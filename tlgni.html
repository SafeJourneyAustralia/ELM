About

Toowoomba's Largest Girls Night In

Get together with the girls for a night of all things ladies love - food, wine and girly giggles. This jam packed evening allows you time with your female family, friends and colleagues to share some laughs over a deli platter and dessert, while enjoying the entertainment, games and raffles. Get Directions

Toowoomba Holden are our event partner for 2018, donating $10 for every ticket we sell to the event. We are extremely grateful for this very generous contribution to our fundraising and are enjoying working with the Toowoomba Holden team. See Ryley and his colleagues at Toowoomba Holden for all your family vehicle needs.
